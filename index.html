Like stars in the sky, every face is unique and the right makeup artist can help you shine from the inside out. Looking your best can transform your mood and confidence in a powerful way. Because of this, I understand that every detail of skin care and makeup application deserves careful consideration.

Having your makeup done is a very personal experience and you want to be sure you're in capable hands. The right makeup artist will recognize your distinctive features and embrace your individuality. As a licensed esthetician and classically trained makeup artist, it is of great importance to me to follow proper sanitation procedures, appropriate uses of skin care, and utilize specific makeup techniques to ensure a safe, beautiful, and long wearing application.

Makeup washes away, but the memories you make will last. When reliving a special day through photos your makeup will remain preserved, timeless, and true to you. There are endless possibilities with makeup and I would be honored to help capture the look that is most authentically you. I hope that together we can manifest your vision and help you not only look, but more importantly, feel like the best version of yourself.
