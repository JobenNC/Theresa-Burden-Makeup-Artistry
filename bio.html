The Artist

From a young age Theresa Ann Burden has always enjoyed expressing herself through many forms of art. She spent many hours immersed in her sidewalk chalk art as a young person and hours playing dress up, applying play makeup, and designing fashion.

The passion of makeup artistry first ignited when I she was receiving her art degree at the University of North Carolina in Asheville. Portraits were her senior focus, utilizing chalk as the medium of choice once again. She always enjoyed applying the makeup to the portrait drawings. As her senior thesis was coming to a close, she started to dabble in costume makeup. I thought to myself “this is really fun and I could see myself enjoying this as a career.”

Theresa’s background in art and design allows her to see faces through an artistic lens. All of the knowledge being an artist greatly informs the techniques, creativity, and makeup transformation. Because Theresa views beauty as an art as well as a science, she found it imperative to return to school once again for her esthetics license.

Her continued learning includes adventures to makeup shows and conventions as far as New York City. I receive training from Master Artists. Through my constant cultivation and eagerness to also improve my craft, I hope to someday travel to Paris, France and visit the head quarters of some my most loved professional brands, such as Viseart. --Which translates to Art of the Face.

With each new face as a fresh canvas, she strives to enhance natural beauty and create a unique look for each person, whether it be for a bride, a birthday girl, a photoshoot, or for a class reunion. No matter what the occasion, Theresa’s personal goal is for you to feel true to your own self, confident, lovely, and the best version of yourself.
